# About Kaira

**Kaira** is a time walker: someone with the ability to move through time without technology, only with her will and a mysterious gift that she doesn't fully understand.

## What is TimeCrawler?

TimeCrawler is a web serial that narrates Kaira's adventures as she travels through different eras of history. Each chapter transports her to a unique moment, from ancient Rome to 1920s Paris, from the Industrial Revolution to the near future.

## Where does Kaira come from?

That's the question Kaira asks herself every day. She woke up one morning in 2026 with the ability to cross the barriers of time, but with no memories of how she obtained that power or who she was before.

## How does it work?

Kaira doesn't travel when she wants. Time calls her, drags her, needs her at specific moments in history. Sometimes to witness, sometimes to intervene, always to learn.

## About the project

TimeCrawler is an experimental narrative project that combines creative writing with technology. Chapters are generated automatically, inspired by real historical events.
